Marine Traffic

The North Sea is the world most busiest area for ship traffic. So it is possible that small ships meet huge container ships. To give you an impression of the ship traffic in the North Sea you can look at the Marine Traffic map below. For ships equiped with an Automatic Identification System (AIS) you can see information about the ships and sometimes even a picture of the ship when you click on the ship symbol
